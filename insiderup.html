Are you ready to #InsiderUp?

We believe that everyone in the world should have the opportunity to learn coding skills, whether they’re a student, happily settled in a job…or think it’s too late to upskill. Our Windows Insider community has of course raised their hands to help make this mission happen for 7.7 billion people across the world.

We’re happy to share our favorite Microsoft learning tools as well as a few experimental Insider-led projects that utilize the latest in Microsoft tech that will help the people you care about learn coding skills: IT pros, military veterans, young people, former full time parents wanting to get back to work, retired folks, … anyone else you think would benefit from upskilling the superpowers you already have.

We’d like to offer you an exclusive opportunity to try these projects in pre-release mode to give us feedback before they roll out to the whole world.

Follow these links to sign up or learn more about the different initiatives:

  • Sign up to hear more about how to become an #InsiderUp community leader: https://aka.ms/InsiderUpLeadReg
  • Sign up to help Alpha test the service: https://aka.ms/CODiERegistration
  • Sign up to be one of the first to test out our new "Project Catnip" PWA: https://aka.ms/ProjectCatnip
  • Sign up to be one of the first to test out and help us create #ITProsCanCode: https://aka.ms/ITProsCanCode
  • Sign up to be one of the first to test out and help us create #VetsCanCode: https://aka.ms/VetsCanCode
  • Learn more about Geeking Out Kids of Color - Intersecting Computer Science & Racial Equity: https://www.GOKiC.org
